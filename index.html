OUR PLANET DEPENDS ON YOU

Join us in our mission to illuminate and protect the wonder of our world

Our global community is composed of philanthropic, passionate, and determined changemakers — like you — from every corner of the globe.

When you become a National Geographic Society member, you have the opportunity to further your impact, affect change, and leave a lasting impression on this earth. Your generosity is a commitment to curiosity — it helps us explore and protect the wonder of our world — all while receiving special benefits.

ANNUAL GIFTS OF $20 - $799

PLATINUM MEMBER

As an Earth Odyssey Platinum Member, your annual gifts support scientific fieldwork and conservation programs around the globe. Your generosity will help us explore and protect our planet’s wildlife and wild places—and in return you will:

  • Receive an annual Earth Odyssey Member Card.
  • Stay in the know with regular updates on our Explorers and programs.
  • Be first to hear about announcements, special features, and opportunities.

ANNUAL GIFTS OF $800 - $25,000

EMERALD MEMBER

Named for one of the first editors of Earth Odyssey magazine, Emerald members play a leading role in some of the most extraordinary science and exploration taking place today. In thanks for your commitment, you’ll receive exclusive, behind-the-scenes access to the Society.

Pioneer ($10,000 - $24,999)

All Explorer, Traiblaizer and Adventurer benefits plus:

  • Complimentary tickets for you and a guest to a Earth Odyssey Society Live event(s) in Washington, D.C.
  • Complimentary membership in the Earth Odyssey Expeditions Returning Traveller Offer program.
  • Curated tour of Earth Odyssey Community Base Camp headquarters for you and your guests.
  • A dedicated Earth Odyssey concierge to assist with personalized experiences.

All Traiblaizer and Adventurer benefits plus:

  • National Geographic magazine gift subscription
  • The Geographer’s Exploration Gift Pack, containing a National Geographic book and other specially selected gifts
  • Receive personal Update from the Field letters with insights into National Geographic Explorers’ current work and new discoveries
  • One-on-one conversation with a National Geographic Society representative to explore your programmatic interests and connect you with experts to answer your questions

All Adventurer benefits plus:

  • Special invites to live online streaming of the Society’s awe-inspiring Explorers Festival
  • Opportunity for complimentary tickets to traveling National Geographic Society exhibitions
  • Personal copy of On Assignment with National Geographic
  • Advance notice of opportunities to travel with top researchers, explorers, writers, and photographers, and presale reservation opportunities for National Geographic Expeditions
  • Complimentary subscription to National Geographic magazine including digital access, the online archive, exclusive ebooks, and popular streaming films
  • Invitations to all exclusive Grosvenor Council Insider Call events
  • Receive exclusive communications detailing the impact of your giving, including leadership emails to be the first to hear about exciting discoveries, bi-annual Impact Reports, and more
  • Your very own official National Geographic Society Yellow Border pin
  • Special recognition of your commitment to our planet with a listing of your name in the National Geographic Society’s Annual Report
  • Discount on purchases at the National Geographic Museum store
